Transport controls (Play / Pause / Stop) over the Cast V2 media namespace,
mirrored to the ongoing notification.

Volume and mute over the Cast V2 receiver namespace, with ±5% step buttons
and a mute toggle. Disables gracefully for fixed-volume receivers.

Material 3 refresh: single Cast surface with top-bar actions for Settings
and Logs, dynamic color (Material You) on Android 12+.

Network security config asserts the app's LAN-only posture — no cleartext
and no trusted CAs for the default HTTP(S) stack, so accidental outbound
Internet traffic fails loudly. Cast TLS uses its own scoped SSLContext
with a TOFU fingerprint pin and is unaffected.
